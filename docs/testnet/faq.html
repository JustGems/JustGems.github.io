Frequent Questions

What is the Blockchain?

A distributed ledger system. A sequence of blocks, or units of digital information, stored consecutively in a public database. The basis for cryptocurrencies.

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for non-fungible token. A unique digital identifier or contract that represents ownership of something on the Blockchain. Fungible means exchangeable one for one. So non-fungible in this context means unique.

What do I do with my NFT? How do I keep it safe?

Just Gems stores your NFT on the blockchain so that you can access it at any time with your crypto wallet. Crypto wallets allow you to sell or purchase NFTs on various marketplaces with crypto currency.

What is a crypto wallet?

A crypto wallet stores cryptocurrencies and NFTs and allows you to transact digitally with other users or markets.

Do I need a crypto wallet to buy NFTs?

Yes on digital marketplaces, and on JustGems.io. When you purchase jewelry on our site, the NFT will be stored in your wallet and can access it at any time. It can also be transferred or sold at your discretion.

Where does my NFT go when I buy it?

If you purchase on our website it is transferred from our account on Opensea to your digital vaullt on the same platform. If you already have a Metamask or other crypto wallet you may purchase directly on our account on Opensea (https://opensea.io/collections/just-gems). And once the transaction is approved the NFT will be visible in your crypto wallet.

What are gas fees?

If you purchase with (cryptocurrency) on Opensea using your Metamask, the Blockchain assesses a transaction fee called "gas fees". These fees range for transferring funds and buying NFTs. Gas fees pay computers for solving equations required to process your transactions securely. This process is open source, which means you can watch what is going on behind the scenes on Etherscan.
